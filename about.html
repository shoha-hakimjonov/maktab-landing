What is Maktab Mobile?

Maktab Mobile is an app with simple easy-to-use interface for distance learning developed for teachers and students.

Maktab Mobile supports video, eBooks, interactive content, allows using and sharing content offline, supports to manage classes and assignments and distributing lesson plans, professional development assignments. Maktab Mobile works both online and offline.

Our Features

Library

Access your coursework, complete assignments, and choose ebooks, interactive exercises or videos from the library to help you learn.

Offline support

Everything works online or offline. Your scores and submissions sync automatically when you reconnect.

Filetype support

Easily add, remove, and edit educational content. Built-in support for your existing content including video, audio, EPUB, PDF, and Experience API files.

Class Management

Create class assignments, track student progress, and record your feedback and grades for students.

Attendance

Manage class details, calendars, and record attendance.

Reports

Efficient, streamlined reporting direct from teachers and students.

Maktab Mobile is the first e-learning portal of the Ministry of Education and Science that works everywhere (computers, tablets, mobile phones) both on-line and off-line promoting learning and competency acquisition.
